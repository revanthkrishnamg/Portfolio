This experience highlighted how analytics can be used to enhance a business, like it did for my channel.

This portfolio showcases a variety of data analytics and machine learning projects (in the projects section) that demonstrate my ability to turn complex data into actionable insights and solutions. Here, you'll find detailed examples of how I've used my skills to tackle real-world problems and drive business success.
